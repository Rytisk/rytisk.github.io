Hi. I'm Rytis Kaplūnas, a husband, father, and software engineer based in Lithuania.

I don't write often, and I don't have much of an online presence - no conference talks, no contributions to open-source projects. Partly because I don't have the time for that, and partly because I simply prefer quiet work.
